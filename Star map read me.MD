# Silver City Celestial Map (Prototype)

**Purpose:** Map the sky’s rhythm from a formal anchor (J2000) to a lived memory (Parade 2025), blending rigor with joy.

**Layers:**
- **J2000 base:** Orbital elements and baseline ellipses.
- **Parade 2025:** Apparent ecliptic alignment, public-friendly icons.
- **Timeline:** Major parades and conjunctions between epochs.

**Contribute:**
- **Data:** Add planetary elements and ephemeris samples.
- **Viz:** Improve ecliptic plots and label spacing.
- **Notes:** Share skywatch logs; keep it lineage-safe and kid-friendly.
