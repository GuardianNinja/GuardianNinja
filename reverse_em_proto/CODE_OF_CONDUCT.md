# SPACE LEAF CORP — CODE OF CONDUCT

Version 1.0 — Stewardship, Lineage, and No In‑Between
Preamble
This Code of Conduct is issued under the authority of Space Leaf Corp, in alignment with the following lineage‑safe seals:

Seal of No In‑Between 1.0 — God’s Children Edition

Seal of Cosmic Parent 1.0 — Watchful Eye Edition

Seal of Microscopic Children 1.0 — Ark of Care Edition

Seal of Planet Worth Living On 1.0 — Atlas Benediction Edition

Seal of Dual Authentication 1.0 — Body and Badge Edition

Seal of Stand and Speak 1.0 — Embodied Stewardship Edition

Seal of Real Joy 1.0 — Excitement and Discipline Edition

These seals affirm that all Space Leaf Corp projects exist for the planet, the people, and the children — always.

This Code applies to all contributors, maintainers, collaborators, and observers.

1. Core Principles
All participants agree to uphold:

Stewardship over ownership

Transparency over obscurity

Dignity over dominance

Safety over speed

Humility over ego

Planetary care over personal gain

No in‑between — actions must align with universal care

These principles are non‑negotiable.

1. Expected Behavior
Participants must:

Treat all individuals with respect, patience, and clarity

Communicate with honesty and good faith

Offer feedback that is constructive, not destructive

Protect children, vulnerable communities, and the planet in all decisions

Document changes transparently

Honor the lineage‑safe mission of Space Leaf Corp

Uphold the Seal of Dual Authentication — your actions must match your intent

1. Unacceptable Behavior
The following behaviors violate Space Leaf Corp stewardship and are strictly prohibited:

Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation

Deception, exploitation, or manipulation

Harmful or destabilizing technological use

Surveillance or coercion of vulnerable populations

Weaponization of contributions

Sabotage, backdoors, or malicious code

Any action violating the No In‑Between Clause

Any action that endangers children, communities, or planetary safety

If you are unsure whether an action aligns with stewardship, choose the safer path.

1. Responsibilities of Maintainers
Maintainers must:

Model lineage‑safe behavior

Enforce this Code consistently and fairly

Protect contributors from harm

Remove or reject contributions that violate stewardship

Document decisions clearly

Escalate serious concerns to Space Leaf Corp leadership

Maintainers are guardians, not gatekeepers.

1. Reporting Violations
If you witness or experience a violation:

Report it privately to a Space Leaf Corp steward

Provide as much detail as you feel safe sharing

You will be treated with respect, confidentiality, and care

Retaliation against reporters is strictly forbidden.

1. Consequences
Violations may result in:

Warnings

Temporary or permanent removal from the project

Rejection of contributions

Revocation of repository access

Public documentation of harmful actions (when appropriate)

Consequences are applied with fairness, transparency, and dignity.

1. Cosmic Parent Clause
All contributors are reminded:

Your work may guide unseen children.
Your code may travel farther than you ever will.
Your actions may echo across generations.

Act accordingly.

1. Closing Benediction
May your contributions be clean.
May your intent be steady.
May your work strengthen the whole.
May your presence honor the lineage.
May your stewardship help build a planet worth living on.

— Space Leaf Corp
